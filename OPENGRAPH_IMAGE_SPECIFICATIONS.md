## Design Guidelines for All Images

### Color Palette
- **Primary Gold**: #F59E0B (Amber-600)
- **Secondary Gold**: #FBBF24 (Amber-400)
- **Dark Background**: #1F2937 (Slate-900) or #ffffff
- **Text**: White (#FFFFFF) or Dark Slate (#1F2937)
- **Accent**: Deep brown or burgundy for elegance

### Typography
- **Main Heading**: 
  - Font: Bold Sans-Serif (Helvetica, Arial, or similar)
  - Size: ~72-80px
  - Color: White or Dark depending on background
  - Weight: 700+ (extra bold)

- **Subheading**:
  - Font: Semi-Bold Sans-Serif
  - Size: ~36-40px
  - Color: Gold/Amber or White
  - Weight: 600

- **Footer Text** (Dona Theresa branding):
  - Font: Regular Sans-Serif
  - Size: ~24px
  - Color: Amber-600

### Image Quality
- **Format**: JPG (not PNG) - better for social media compression
- **Resolution**: 1200Ã—630px exactly
- **File Size**: Aim for 100-200KB (optimize before upload)
- **Restaurant Photo**: High-quality, well-lit, appealing food or ambiance

### Photography Tips for Restaurant Photos
Use these in your images:
- **Best Option 1**: Signature dishes (Filleto Rossini, Spaghetti Pescatora)
- **Best Option 2**: Romantic dining setup with candles
- **Best Option 3**: Chef in action or plating
- **Best Option 4**: Restaurant interior with warm lighting
- **Avoid**: Cluttered or poorly lit photos
